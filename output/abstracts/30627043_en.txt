Six new species are described in the large Neotropical genus <i>Paullinia</i> (Sapindaceae), <i>P.cidii</i>, <i>P.decorticans</i>, <i>P.fruticosa</i>, <i>P.hondurensis</i>; <i>P.martinellii</i> and <i>P.wurdackii</i>. In addition, they are illustrated and contrasted to the morphologically most similar species currently known. The new species were discovered while working on a forthcoming revision of the genus.
