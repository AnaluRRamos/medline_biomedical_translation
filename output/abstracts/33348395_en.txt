To find out which was the opinion of residents in obstetrics and gynecology about the advantages and disadvantages of medical abortion as compared with surgical procedures.  Cross-sectional multicenter study among residents in obstetrics and gynecology from 21 maternity hospitals located in 4 different geographical regions of Brazil, using a self-responded questionnaire with 31 questions related to their opinion and experience on providing abortion services.  Most residents agreed that 'being less invasive' (94.7%), 'does not require anesthesia' (89.7%), 'can be accompanied during the process' (89.1%), 'prevents physical trauma' (84.4%) were the main advantages of medical abortion.  Residents perceived both clinical and personal issues as advantages of medical abortion.
