To evaluate the knowledge and behaviors of health professionals of units of the Strategies Family opposite gender violence. This descriptive study with 53 seven of units of the family health strategy professionals from March to July 2015. Data were collected through a self-administered instrument and analyzed in Excel 2007. It was observed that the knowledge of professionals about definitions, epidemiology and management of violence ranged from reasonable to good, despite knowing little about the prevalence of violence during pregnancy. Regarding the conduct was evident difficulty in questioning women about violence and its notification. Professionals with shorter assistance and who received training were more assertive results regarding conduct. It is suggested that educational actions in service be carried out in order to provide subsidies for the professionals' action against cases of gender violence.
