To evaluate the fellow in knee surgery and the training offered by accredited programs in Brazil. This cross-sectional study administered a questionnaire to fellows in knee surgery. Most fellows reported being assisted by the preceptor in theoretical, practical, and scientific activities. The training offered by accredited programs to fellows in knee surgery presented good results for their education. This study may propose educational measures for the Brazilian Society for Surgery of the Knee (SBCJ). <b><i>Level of Evidence IIC, Cross-sectional study.</i></b>
