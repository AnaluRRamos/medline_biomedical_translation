Local anaesthesia for procedures involving the distal limb are a useful component of balanced protocols to achieve effective procedural analgesia for patients. A ring block is an effective and straightforward technique. To review and describe the ring block technique for local anaesthesia of the canine and feline distal limb. Images of hospitalized patients were used. These photographs were obtained with owner permission. The clinical technique for the ring block of the canine or feline distal limb is described. The ring block can be implemented simply and successfully for a variety of procedures involving the distal limb to provide effective analgesia.
