to translate and cross-culturally adapt the Behavior Change Protocol for educational practices in Diabetes Mellitus. methodological study aimed at cross-cultural adaptation, comprising the steps of translation, back-translation, assessment by an expert committee and pre-testing of the instrument on a sample of 30 healthcare service users with type 2 Diabetes Mellitus. the instrument was assessed based on criteria pertaining semantic, idiomatic, conceptual and cultural equivalence between the original instrument and the translated version, its mean Content Validity Index being 0.85. results showed content validity indicating the instrument's successful cross-cultural adaptation to the Brazilian culture for use in educational practices targeting self-care in type 2 DM.
