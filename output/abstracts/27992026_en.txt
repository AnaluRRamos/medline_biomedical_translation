The CFA has established the validity of the factorial structure. The internal consistency of the scale was considered adequate (Î±=0.85) presenting a sensitivity of 70% and specificity of 75%. the EAFAA constitutes a reliable instrument to identify the attitudes of nursing students towards alcohol, alcoholism and persons with disorders related to alcohol use.
